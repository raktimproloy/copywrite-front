Having a sound technical SEO strategy is critical to the success of any online business. After all, if your website isn't properly optimized for search engines, then it's likely that potential customers will never even find you in the first place.

Luckily, achieving technical SEO success is not nearly as difficult as it may seem at first glance. By following a few simple tips and best practices, you can ensure that your website is well-positioned to rank highly in SERPs.

In this blog post, we'll share some top tips for achieving technical SEO success. We'll cover topics like choosing the right keywords, optimizing your website's structure, and improving your site's overall performance. By the time you're finished reading, you'll have everything you need to take your technical SEO game to the next level!

Search and purchase your preferred domain

Choosing the right domain name is one of the most important steps in setting up your website. Your domain name is how people will find you online, so it's important to choose one that is easy to remember and spells out what your business is about.

There are a few different things to consider when choosing your domain name:

  • It should be easy to spell and pronounce
  • It should be relevant to your business
  • It should be short and memorable
  • It should be available as a .com
If you're having trouble coming up with a domain name, try brainstorming with a friend or using a domain name generator. Once you've found a few potential names, check to see if they're available as a .com by doing a quick search online. Once you've found your perfect domain name, you can purchase it through a domain registrar like GoDaddy or Namecheap .

Select a hosting provider for your website

There are many things to consider when selecting a hosting provider for your website. The most important factor to consider is whether the provider offers the features and services that you need. Some of the features to look for include unlimited storage, bandwidth, email accounts, and a free domain name. Another important factor to consider is the provider's customer support. Make sure that the provider offers 24/7 customer support so that you can always get help when you need it.

Create a website structure that is easy to navigate and understand

One of the most important aspects of SEO that is often overlooked is the structure of a website. Having a well-defined framework and site structure can improve your ranking, and authority, and make it easier for search engines to crawl your pages. It can also create a better overall user experience.

Configure a sitemap for your website

Sitemap files help search engines index your website and understand its structure. By including a sitemap file on your website, you're providing search engines with valuable information that can help them understand your site better and improve their search results.

Here are some tips for creating and submitting a sitemap file for your website:

  1. Use a sitemap generator tool to create your sitemap file. There are many free and paid sitemap generator tools available online.
  2. Make sure your sitemap file is well-formed and valid. You can use a tool like the W3C Markup Validation Service to check your sitemap file.
  3. Submit your sitemap file to search engines. Submit a Site map on all major site engine.
    • Google Search Console
    • Bing Webmaster Tools
    • Yandex Webmaster Tools
  4. Keep your sitemap file up to date. As you make changes to your website, be sure to update your sitemap file accordingly.

Use Friendly Permalinks

One important tip for technical SEO success is to use friendly permalinks. A permalink is the web address used to link to your blog post or web page. It is important to use descriptive and keyword-rich permalinks so that search engines can easily index and rank your content. Additionally, using friendly permalinks will make it easier for users to find and share your content.

Request Crawling for Faster Indexing

By default, Googlebot crawls the Internet with a particular focus on new websites and pages. However, if you want your website or specific pages to be indexed more quickly, you can submit a request to Google for them to be crawled. There are two ways to do this: first, you can use the Fetch as Google tool in Search Console, which allows you to request individual URLs; or second, you can submit a sitemap, which is a file that contains a list of all the URLs on your website. Both of these methods are quick and easy to do, and they can help to speed up the process of getting your website indexed. In addition, if you have a large website or if your website is updated frequently, submitting a sitemap can be a helpful way to ensure that all of your pages are included in the Google index.

Perform a search engine optimization audit each month

It's important to stay on top of your website's SEO health by conducting monthly audits. This will help you catch any issues that may have arisen and fix them before they cause serious problems. Here are some tips for conducting effective monthly audits:

  1. Choose a day or week that you will dedicate to your audit. This way, you won't have to worry about fitting it into your busy schedule.
  2. Make a list of all the things you need to check. This may include things like your website's code, structure, content, links, etc.
  3. Take your time and go through each item on your list. Don't rush the process, as you may miss something important.
  4. Keep a log of any issues you find. This will help you track your progress and see which areas need improvement.
  5. Make a plan to fix any issues you find. This may involve making changes to your website's code, structure, content, etc.
By following these tips, you can ensure that your website's SEO is in good health.
