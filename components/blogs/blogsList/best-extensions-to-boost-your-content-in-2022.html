1. Grammarly

Grammarly is a software application that helps users improve their writing skills. The program utilizes artificial intelligence and machine learning to identify grammar mistakes and suggest corrections. In addition to correcting grammar errors, Grammarly can also help users find appropriate synonyms for words they are using, detect plagiarism, and suggest style improvements.

2. MozBar

MozBar is a free toolbar and extension for Chrome, Firefox, and Opera that allows you to see how well pages rank in Google search results. With MozBar, you can quickly assess the Page Authority and Domain Authority of any page, as well as view key link metrics such as Link Influence Scores and Total Links.

3. Keyword Surfer

Keyword Surfer is a free online keyword research tool that allows users to find keywords related to their website or business. The tool provides data on monthly search volume, competition level, and other important metrics for each keyword. Keyword Surfer also offers a variety of other features, including the ability to save keyword lists, track competitor rankings, and receive weekly email alerts with new keyword suggestions.

4. SEOquake

SEOquake is a free Chrome, Firefox, and Opera extension that provides SEO metrics for the current web page and offers site analysis tools. The extension scrapes data from a variety of sources, including Moz, Majestic, and Ahrefs, and displays key SEO metrics in the browser toolbar. These metrics include Domain Authority (DA), Page Authority (PA), backlinks, social shares, and others. SEOquake also has a search bar that can be used to find specific pages on a website or competitors website.

5 Ubersuggest

Ubersuggest is a free online keyword research tool that offers users a wealth of data on the popularity and use of specific keywords. The tool can help you identify keywords that are relevant to your business or organization, and that have a high potential for traffic and conversion. Ubersuggest allows you to see how people are using keywords across the web, so you can gauge whether they would be a good fit for your marketing efforts. You can also get information on search volume, competition, and other factors that can help you choose the right keywords for your campaign.

6. WhiteSmoke

WhiteSmoke is a great tool for writers of all levels. It can help you improve your grammar, spelling, and writing style. It can also help you with your punctuation and sentence structure. WhiteSmoke is a great tool for helping you to edit your work, and it can also help you to proofread your work.
